Veriflex PUR Industrial Ethernet Cable

APPLICATION

Veriflex Industrial Ethernet cable suitable for Profinet Type
B applications. Can be used in dry or damp rooms for plant
engineering and machinery manufacturing. The rugged
polyurethane sheath provides protection even in extreme
industrial environments
