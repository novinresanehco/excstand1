Veriflex YY LSZH (HSLH) Control Cable

APPLICATION

Veriflex low smoke halogen-free flexible connecting cable for instrumentation and control equipment for tooling machinery, production lines, and in flexible applications with free movement and no tensile load. Suitable for use in dry, ambient and wet rooms. These cables are not suitable for outdoor or underground installations..
