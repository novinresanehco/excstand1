BS 7846 F120 Armoured
Fire Performance Power Cable

APPLICATION

These armoured fire performance power cables are fire
resistant and designed for direct impact. Designed to meet
the application of fire, direct impact and water jet as specified
in BS 8491, making them suitable for use as 120 minute rated
category 3 power cables according to BS 8519.
STANDARDSBS 7846-F120
Fire Resistant to: BS 8491-120 mins, BS 6387 CAT CWZ, BS 8519 Cat3
Flame Retardant to: IEC 60332-3 Cat C, IEC 60332-1-2
Low Smoke Zero Halogen to: IEC 60754-1/2, IEC 61034-1/2
